# Educational Carousel Creator

This skill helps businesses create informative and visually appealing carousels for social media.

## Instructions

### Step 1: Choose a Topic and Outline the Content

- Select a topic that is relevant to the user's business and valuable to their target audience.
- Break down the topic into 5-10 bite-sized tips, steps, or ideas.
- Each point will become a slide in the carousel.

### Step 2: Design the Carousel Slides

- **Slide 1 (Hook):** A compelling headline and an eye-catching image to grab attention.
- **Slides 2-9 (Content):** Each slide should focus on a single point, with a clear heading, concise text, and a relevant visual (icon, photo, or illustration).
- **Slide 10 (Call to Action):** A clear call-to-action, such as "Save this post for later," "Share with a friend," or "Click the link in our bio."

### Step 3: Write the Carousel Copy

- Write a caption that provides additional context and encourages engagement.
- Include relevant hashtags to increase reach.
- Tag any relevant accounts.

### Step 4: Produce Deliverables

- Create a Markdown document titled `carousel_content.md`.
- The document should include:
    - The text for each slide of the carousel.
    - The caption for the post.
    - A list of recommended hashtags.
    - Suggestions for visuals for each slide.

## Examples

### Example Input

"I'm a real estate agent and I want to create an Instagram carousel about the home buying process."

### Example Output

A `carousel_content.md` file that outlines a 10-slide carousel about the home buying process. The slides would cover topics such as getting pre-approved for a mortgage, finding a real estate agent, making an offer, and closing the deal. The final slide would have a call-to-action to "Contact me for a free consultation."
