# Local SEO Optimizer

This skill helps businesses optimize their online presence for local search.

## Instructions

### Step 1: Conduct a Local SEO Audit

- Use the `local-business-audit` skill to conduct a comprehensive audit of the business's online presence.
- Identify areas for improvement in their Google Business Profile, on-page SEO, and local citations.

### Step 2: Optimize Google Business Profile

- Use the `google-business-profile-optimizer` skill to fully optimize the business's GBP listing.
- This includes completing all sections of the profile, adding high-quality photos, and generating positive reviews.

### Step 3: On-Page SEO Optimization

- **Keyword Research:** Identify relevant local keywords (e.g., "plumber in [city]").
- **Content Optimization:** Incorporate local keywords into the website's content, title tags, and meta descriptions.
- **NAP Consistency:** Ensure the business's name, address, and phone number (NAP) are consistent across the website.
- **Create Location Pages:** If the business has multiple locations, create a separate page for each location with unique content.

### Step 4: Local Link Building

- **Citation Building:** Build citations on relevant local and industry directories.
- **Local Outreach:** Reach out to other local businesses, bloggers, and news outlets to acquire backlinks.

### Step 5: Produce Deliverables

- Create a Markdown document titled `local_seo_plan.md`.
- The document should include:
    - A summary of the local SEO audit.
    - A list of recommended optimizations for the Google Business Profile and website.
    - A plan for building local citations and backlinks.
    - A list of KPIs to track progress (e.g., keyword rankings, website traffic, leads).

## Examples

### Example Input

"I want to improve my dental practice's ranking on Google Maps."

### Example Output

A `local_seo_plan.md` file that provides a detailed plan for improving the dental practice's local SEO. The plan would include recommendations for optimizing their Google Business Profile, building local citations, and creating location-specific content on their website.
