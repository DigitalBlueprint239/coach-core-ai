# Local Business Audit

This skill conducts a thorough audit of a local business's online presence to provide actionable recommendations for improvement.

## Instructions

### Step 1: Gather Business Information

- Ask the user for the following information:
    - Business Name
    - Business Address
    - Business Website
    - Target geographic area

### Step 2: Audit the Online Presence

- **Google Business Profile (GBP) Audit:**
    - Search for the business on Google Maps to find their GBP listing.
    - Check for completeness of the profile (address, phone number, hours, photos, etc.).
    - Analyze customer reviews and the business's responses.
    - Review the Q&A section.
- **Local SEO Audit:**
    - Use web search to check the business's rankings for relevant local keywords (e.g., "plumber in [city]").
    - Analyze their on-page SEO (title tags, meta descriptions, local keywords in content).
    - Check for local business citations (listings in online directories like Yelp, Yellow Pages, etc.).
- **Website Audit:**
    - Review the website for mobile-friendliness, page speed, and overall user experience.
    - Check for clear calls-to-action and easy-to-find contact information.
- **Online Reputation Audit:**
    - Search for the business name on review sites (Yelp, TripAdvisor, etc.) and social media.
    - Analyze the sentiment of reviews and comments.

### Step 3: Synthesize Findings and Generate Recommendations

- Identify the business's strengths and weaknesses in each area of the audit.
- Prioritize recommendations based on their potential impact and ease of implementation.
- Create a list of actionable steps the business can take to improve its local marketing.

### Step 4: Produce Deliverables

- Create a Markdown document titled `local_business_audit.md`.
- The report should include:
    - A summary of the audit findings.
    - A detailed breakdown of each section of the audit (GBP, Local SEO, Website, Reputation).
    - A prioritized list of actionable recommendations.

## Examples

### Example Input

"I own a restaurant in San Francisco. Can you audit my online presence and tell me how to get more customers?"

### Example Output

A `local_business_audit.md` file that provides a comprehensive audit of the restaurant's online presence. The report would highlight areas for improvement, such as an incomplete Google Business Profile, a lack of customer reviews, and a non-mobile-friendly website. It would also provide a list of recommendations, such as optimizing their GBP listing, encouraging customers to leave reviews, and redesigning their website to be mobile-responsive.
