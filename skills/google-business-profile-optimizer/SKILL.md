# Google Business Profile Optimizer

This skill helps businesses optimize their Google Business Profile (GBP) to attract more local customers.

## Instructions

### Step 1: Complete and Update Profile Information

- **NAP Consistency:** Ensure the business's name, address, and phone number (NAP) are accurate and consistent with their website and other online listings.
- **Categories:** Choose the most relevant primary and secondary categories for the business.
- **Services/Products:** Add a detailed list of services and products, with descriptions and prices.
- **Business Hours:** Keep business hours accurate, including special hours for holidays.
- **From the Business:** Write a compelling business description that highlights the unique value proposition.

### Step 2: Enhance the Profile with Media

- **Photos:** Upload high-quality photos of the business's exterior, interior, products, services, and team.
- **Videos:** Add short videos to showcase the business's personality and offerings.

### Step 3: Engage with Customers

- **Reviews:** Encourage customers to leave reviews and respond to all reviews (both positive and negative) in a professional and timely manner.
- **Q&A:** Monitor and answer questions in the Q&A section.
- **Google Posts:** Regularly create Google Posts to share updates, offers, and events.

### Step 4: Produce Deliverables

- Create a Markdown document titled `gbp_optimization_plan.md`.
- The document should include:
    - A checklist of recommended optimizations for the GBP listing.
    - A plan for generating and responding to reviews.
    - A content calendar for Google Posts.

## Examples

### Example Input

"I need to optimize my restaurant's Google Business Profile to attract more diners."

### Example Output

A `gbp_optimization_plan.md` file that provides a detailed plan for optimizing the restaurant's GBP. The plan would include recommendations for completing all sections of the profile, uploading high-quality photos of the food and ambiance, and creating a strategy for encouraging and responding to customer reviews.
