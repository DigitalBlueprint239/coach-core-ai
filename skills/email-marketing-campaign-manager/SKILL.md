# Email Marketing Campaign Manager

This skill helps businesses manage their email marketing campaigns to nurture leads and drive sales.

## Instructions

### Step 1: Segment the Email List

- Segment the email list based on demographics, interests, and purchase history.
- This will allow for more targeted and personalized email campaigns.

### Step 2: Plan the Email Campaign

- **Campaign Goal:** What is the goal of the campaign (e.g., nurture leads, promote a new product, announce a sale)?
- **Email Sequence:** If it's a multi-part campaign, map out the sequence of emails.
- **Content:** Write the copy for each email, including a compelling subject line, engaging body copy, and a clear call-to-action.

### Step 3: Design the Email Template

- Design a professional and mobile-responsive email template that is consistent with the user's brand.
- Use a mix of text, images, and white space to create a visually appealing layout.

### Step 4: Test and Send the Campaign

- **A/B Testing:** Test different subject lines, calls-to-action, and email designs to see what performs best.
- **Sending:** Schedule the email campaign to be sent at the optimal time for the target audience.

### Step 5: Produce Deliverables

- Create a Markdown document titled `email_campaign_plan.md`.
- The document should include:
    - The email campaign goals and target audience.
    - The email copy for each email in the sequence.
    - The email design template.
    - A plan for A/B testing and sending the campaign.

## Examples

### Example Input

"I want to create an email campaign to welcome new subscribers to my email list."

### Example Output

An `email_campaign_plan.md` file that outlines a 3-part welcome email series. The first email would welcome the new subscriber and deliver the lead magnet. The second email would introduce the brand and its story. The third email would showcase the brand's most popular products or services.
