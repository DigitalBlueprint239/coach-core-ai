# Competitor Analysis Engine

This skill provides a comprehensive analysis of a business's competitors.

## Instructions

### Step 1: Identify Competitors

- Ask the user to provide a list of their main competitors.
- If the user is unsure, use web search to identify competitors based on the user's industry and location.
- Prioritize direct competitors (those offering similar products or services to the same target market).

### Step 2: Analyze Competitor's Online Presence

- **Website Analysis:**
    - Review the competitor's website for its design, user experience, messaging, and calls-to-action.
    - Identify the target audience and value proposition.
    - Check for a blog, case studies, and other content marketing efforts.
- **SEO Analysis:**
    - Use web search to identify the keywords the competitor ranks for.
    - Analyze their backlink profile to understand their off-page SEO strategy.
- **Social Media Analysis:**
    - Review the competitor's social media profiles (Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn, etc.).
    - Analyze their content strategy, engagement rates, and follower growth.
- **Content Marketing Analysis:**
    - Identify the types of content the competitor is creating (blog posts, videos, podcasts, etc.).
    - Analyze the quality, frequency, and topics of their content.

### Step 3: Synthesize Findings

- Create a SWOT analysis (Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, Threats) for each competitor.
- Identify each competitor's unique selling proposition (USP).
- Compare the user's business to its competitors across key areas (pricing, product features, marketing, etc.).

### Step 4: Produce Deliverables

- Create a Markdown document titled `competitor_analysis_report.md`.
- The report should include:
    - An overview of the competitive landscape.
    - Detailed profiles of each competitor, including their SWOT analysis and USP.
    - A competitive matrix comparing the user's business to its competitors.
    - Actionable recommendations for the user to improve their competitive position.

## Examples

### Example Input

"I'm opening a new coffee shop in my neighborhood. Can you analyze my top 3 competitors?"

### Example Output

A `competitor_analysis_report.md` file that provides a detailed analysis of the three competing coffee shops. The report would include their strengths (e.g., strong brand, loyal customer base), weaknesses (e.g., high prices, poor location), and marketing strategies. It would also offer recommendations for the new coffee shop, such as focusing on a specific niche (e.g., specialty coffee, vegan pastries) or offering a unique customer experience.
