# Viral Short-Form Video Generator

This skill helps businesses create engaging short-form videos that have the potential to go viral.

## Instructions

### Step 1: Brainstorm Video Concepts

- Research trending topics, sounds, and formats on the target platform (TikTok, Instagram Reels, etc.).
- Brainstorm video ideas that are relevant to the user's business and target audience.
- Focus on concepts that are entertaining, educational, or inspiring.

### Step 2: Choose a Video Format

- Select a video format that is likely to perform well, such as:
    - **How-to/Tutorials:** Teach the audience how to do something.
    - **Behind-the-Scenes:** Show a glimpse into the business.
    - **User-Generated Content (UGC):** Feature videos from customers.
    - **Trend-Jacking:** Participate in a popular trend.
    - **Storytelling:** Tell a compelling story about the brand or a customer.

### Step 3: Write the Video Script

- Write a script that is concise, engaging, and easy to follow.
- The script should have a strong hook to grab the viewer's attention in the first 3 seconds.
- Include a clear call-to-action (e.g., "Follow for more tips," "Link in bio to shop").
- Keep the video length between 15 and 60 seconds.

### Step 4: Produce Deliverables

- Create a Markdown document titled `video_scripts.md`.
- The document should include:
    - A list of 5-10 video ideas with brief descriptions.
    - A detailed script for one of the video ideas, including visual and audio cues.
    - Recommendations for hashtags, music, and editing style.

## Examples

### Example Input

"I want to create some TikTok videos for my online clothing boutique. I sell trendy and affordable clothes for young women."

### Example Output

A `video_scripts.md` file with several video ideas, such as a "style hack" video showing how to wear a basic t-shirt in five different ways, a "day in the life of a boutique owner" video, and a video showcasing new arrivals. The file would also include a detailed script for one of the ideas, complete with suggestions for trending audio and on-screen text.
