# Brand Positioning Architect

This skill helps businesses define their brand positioning and create a powerful brand strategy.

## Instructions

### Step 1: Understand the Business and its Goals

- Analyze the business's mission, vision, and values.
- Understand the business's target audience and the problems it solves for them.
- Clarify the business's long-term goals.

### Step 2: Analyze the Market and Competition

- Use the `competitor-analysis-engine` skill to analyze the competitive landscape.
- Identify the positioning of key competitors.
- Identify gaps in the market that the business can fill.

### Step 3: Define the Brand's Unique Value Proposition (UVP)

- Based on the analysis, brainstorm what makes the business unique.
- The UVP should be a clear and concise statement that answers the question: "Why should a customer choose you over your competitors?"
- The UVP should focus on a key benefit that is important to the target audience.

### Step 4: Develop the Brand Positioning Statement

- Create a brand positioning statement using the following template:

> For [target audience], [brand name] is the [category/frame of reference] that [UVP/key benefit] because [reason to believe].

### Step 5: Produce Deliverables

- Create a Markdown document titled `brand_positioning_strategy.md`.
- The document should include:
    - The brand's UVP.
    - The brand positioning statement.
    - A summary of the target audience, competitive landscape, and market gaps.
    - Recommendations for how to communicate the brand positioning across all marketing channels.

## Examples

### Example Input

"I'm launching a new line of organic dog food. How can I position my brand to stand out?"

### Example Output

A `brand_positioning_strategy.md` file that defines a unique position for the organic dog food brand. The strategy might focus on the use of locally sourced, human-grade ingredients, and target health-conscious dog owners who are willing to pay a premium for high-quality pet food. The positioning statement could be: "For health-conscious dog owners, 'Pawsitively Pure' is the only dog food that provides a truly farm-to-bowl experience because we source all our ingredients from local, organic farms."
