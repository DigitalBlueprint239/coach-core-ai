# Customer Avatar Builder

This skill helps businesses create detailed customer avatars, providing a clear picture of their ideal customer.

## Instructions

### Step 1: Analyze the Business

- Review the business's website, products, and services to understand its core offerings.
- Identify the primary value proposition and the problems the business solves for its customers.

### Step 2: Gather Customer Data

- If available, analyze existing customer data (demographics, purchase history, feedback).
- If no data is available, conduct research on the target industry and identify typical customer profiles.
- Use web search to find forums, social media groups, and reviews related to the business's niche to understand customer pain points, desires, and language.

### Step 3: Generate the Customer Avatar

- Based on the gathered data, create a detailed customer avatar with the following sections:
    - **Demographics:** Age, gender, location, income, education, family status.
    - **Psychographics:** Values, beliefs, interests, lifestyle, personality.
    - **Goals & Challenges:** What are their primary goals and the challenges they face in achieving them?
    - **Pain Points:** What problems do they need to solve?
    - **Watering Holes:** Where do they hang out online and offline (blogs, forums, social media, events)?
    - **A Day in the Life:** Describe a typical day for the avatar.
    - **Quotes:** Include a few quotes that capture the avatar's voice and perspective.

### Step 4: Produce Deliverables

- Create a Markdown document titled `customer_avatar.md`.
- The document should present the customer avatar in a clear and organized format.
- Include a summary of key insights and how the business can use this avatar to improve its marketing efforts.

## Examples

### Example Input

"I run a local gym and I want to attract more members. Can you help me understand my ideal customer?"

### Example Output

A `customer_avatar.md` file containing a detailed customer avatar for a local gym, including demographics, psychographics, goals, challenges, and pain points. The output would also include recommendations on how the gym can tailor its marketing messages and services to attract this ideal customer.
