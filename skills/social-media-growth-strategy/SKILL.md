# Social Media Growth Strategy

This skill helps businesses develop a data-driven strategy for growing their social media presence.

## Instructions

### Step 1: Set SMART Goals

- Work with the user to set Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, and Time-bound (SMART) goals for their social media.
- Examples of SMART goals:
    - Increase Instagram followers by 20% in the next 3 months.
    - Double the engagement rate on Facebook in the next quarter.
    - Generate 50 leads from LinkedIn in the next month.

### Step 2: Choose the Right Platforms

- Identify the social media platforms where the user's target audience is most active.
- Consider the user's industry, business goals, and resources.
- It's better to be active on 1-2 platforms than to be spread too thin across multiple platforms.

### Step 3: Develop a Content Strategy

- Create a content calendar that outlines the topics, formats, and frequency of posts.
- The content should be a mix of promotional, educational, and entertaining content.
- Use the `viral-short-form-video-generator` and `educational-carousel-creator` skills to create engaging content.

### Step 4: Create an Engagement Strategy

- Outline a plan for interacting with followers, responding to comments and messages, and participating in relevant conversations.
- Encourage user-generated content (UGC) by running contests, featuring customer photos, and creating branded hashtags.

### Step 5: Produce Deliverables

- Create a Markdown document titled `social_media_growth_strategy.md`.
- The document should include:
    - The SMART goals for social media.
    - The recommended social media platforms.
    - A detailed content strategy and content calendar.
    - An engagement strategy.
    - A list of key performance indicators (KPIs) to track progress.

## Examples

### Example Input

"I want to grow my coaching business on Instagram. Can you help me create a growth strategy?"

### Example Output

A `social_media_growth_strategy.md` file that outlines a comprehensive growth strategy for the coaching business on Instagram. The strategy would include goals such as increasing followers and generating leads, a content plan focused on providing value to the target audience, and an engagement plan for building a community. The report would also include a list of KPIs to measure success.
