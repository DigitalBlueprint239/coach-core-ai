# Paid Ad Campaign Builder

This skill helps businesses create and launch effective paid advertising campaigns.

## Instructions

### Step 1: Define Campaign Goals and Budget

- **Campaign Goal:** What is the primary objective of the campaign (e.g., brand awareness, lead generation, sales)?
- **Budget:** What is the total budget for the campaign, and how will it be allocated (e.g., daily, lifetime)?
- **KPIs:** What key performance indicators will be used to measure success (e.g., cost per click, conversion rate, return on ad spend)?

### Step 2: Define the Target Audience

- Use the `customer-avatar-builder` skill to define the target audience.
- Create a detailed audience profile based on demographics, interests, and behaviors.

### Step 3: Choose Ad Platforms and Formats

- **Platforms:** Select the most appropriate ad platforms based on the target audience and campaign goals (e.g., Facebook, Instagram, Google, LinkedIn).
- **Formats:** Choose the ad formats that are most likely to resonate with the target audience (e.g., image ads, video ads, carousel ads, search ads).

### Step 4: Create Ad Copy and Creatives

- **Ad Copy:** Write compelling ad copy that speaks directly to the target audience and highlights the unique value proposition.
- **Creatives:** Design eye-catching visuals (images or videos) that are optimized for each ad platform.

### Step 5: Produce Deliverables

- Create a Markdown document titled `paid_ad_campaign_plan.md`.
- The document should include:
    - The campaign goals, budget, and KPIs.
    - The target audience profile.
    - The recommended ad platforms and formats.
    - The ad copy and creative concepts.
    - A plan for testing and optimizing the campaign.

## Examples

### Example Input

"I want to run a Facebook ad campaign to promote my new online course. My budget is $500."

### Example Output

A `paid_ad_campaign_plan.md` file that outlines a complete Facebook ad campaign for the online course. The plan would include a specific target audience (e.g., young professionals interested in digital marketing), ad copy and creative ideas, and a testing plan to optimize the campaign for conversions.
