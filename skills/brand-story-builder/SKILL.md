# Brand Story Builder

This skill helps businesses create a compelling brand story that resonates with their target audience.

## Instructions

### Step 1: Uncover the Core Narrative

- **The "Why":** Why was the business started? What is its purpose beyond making money?
- **The Founder's Story:** What is the personal story of the founder? What struggles did they overcome?
- **The Customer's Transformation:** How does the business help its customers transform their lives?

### Step 2: Structure the Brand Story

- Use a classic storytelling framework, such as the Hero's Journey:
    - **The Hero:** The customer.
    - **The Guide:** The brand.
    - **The Problem:** The challenge the customer is facing.
    - **The Plan:** The solution the brand provides.
    - **The Call to Action:** The step the customer needs to take.
    - **The Success:** The positive outcome the customer will experience.
    - **The Failure:** The negative consequences of not taking action.

### Step 3: Write the Brand Story

- Write a short, engaging narrative that incorporates the elements from the previous steps.
- Use the brand's voice and personality.
- Focus on emotion and connection, not just features and benefits.

### Step 4: Produce Deliverables

- Create a Markdown document titled `brand_story.md`.
- The document should include:
    - The full brand story.
    - A shorter version that can be used as an "elevator pitch."
    - Recommendations for how to use the brand story in marketing materials (e.g., on the website's "About Us" page, in social media bios, in email marketing campaigns).

## Examples

### Example Input

"I want to create a brand story for my handmade soap company. We use all-natural ingredients and our mission is to promote self-care."

### Example Output

A `brand_story.md` file that tells the story of the soap company. The story might focus on the founder's personal journey to a more natural lifestyle and her desire to share the gift of self-care with others. The hero of the story would be the customer who is looking for a way to de-stress and pamper themselves. The soap would be positioned as the guide that helps them achieve this transformation.
