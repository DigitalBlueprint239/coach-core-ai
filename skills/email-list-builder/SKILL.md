# Email List Builder

This skill helps businesses build a valuable email list of potential customers.

## Instructions

### Step 1: Create a Compelling Lead Magnet

- **Lead Magnet:** A free resource that is offered in exchange for an email address.
- Brainstorm lead magnet ideas that are highly valuable to the user's target audience, such as:
    - Ebooks
    - Checklists
    - Templates
    - Webinars
    - Free trials

### Step 2: Design a High-Converting Opt-in Form

- **Opt-in Form:** A form on a website where visitors can sign up for the email list.
- Design an opt-in form that is simple, clear, and easy to use.
- Include a strong call-to-action that clearly communicates the benefit of signing up.

### Step 3: Promote the Lead Magnet

- **Website:** Promote the lead magnet on the user's website with pop-ups, slide-ins, and in-line forms.
- **Social Media:** Share the lead magnet on social media and run ads to drive traffic to the opt-in page.
- **Content Marketing:** Create blog posts, videos, and other content that promotes the lead magnet.

### Step 4: Produce Deliverables

- Create a Markdown document titled `email_list_building_plan.md`.
- The document should include:
    - A list of lead magnet ideas.
    - A design for a high-converting opt-in form.
    - A plan for promoting the lead magnet.

## Examples

### Example Input

"I want to build an email list for my new SaaS product."

### Example Output

An `email_list_building_plan.md` file that outlines a strategy for building an email list for the SaaS product. The plan would include ideas for lead magnets (e.g., a free trial, a white paper), a design for an opt-in form, and a promotional plan that includes content marketing and social media.
